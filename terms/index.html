    East Coast Rationalist Megameetup 2023

    For 2023, our venue is the Brooklyn Downtown Holiday Inn. Concisely, if at any point the hotel asks us to remove a guest, the NYC Megameetup team will cheerfully ask that guest to leave. We may or may not refund you, depending primarily on whether the hotel refunds us.

    The meeting space and the room are more separate this year than in previous years. If you are behaving disruptively in the meeting spaces or making people sufficiently uncomfortable, the NYC Megameetup team may ask you to leave the meeting spaces for the duration of the weekend.

    If you see a problem, please point it out to the NYC Megameetup team.

    -Skyler Crossman
