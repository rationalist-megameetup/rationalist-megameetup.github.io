    NYC Rationalist Megameetup 2025

    For 2025, our venue is the HI NYC Hostel. Concisely: if at any point the hostel asks us to remove a guest, the NYC Megameetup team will cheerfully ask that guest to leave. We may or may not refund you, depending primarily on whether the hostel refunds us.

    The NYC Megameetup team may also ask you to leave the conference spaces for our own reasons, primarily if you're doing something disruptive or offensive. We're still small enough that the code of conduct is close to "does this make the organizer team sad or unhappy to have you here?" Don't make the organizers sad.

    If you see a problem, please point it out to the NYC Megameetup team. If you think you can fix the problem on your own, go for it!
