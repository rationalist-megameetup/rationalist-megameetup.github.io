    We look forward to seeing you! Please note that the tickets don't overlap- if you want to go to Megameetup, stay overnight, and go to solstice, you'll want one of each.

    • Solstice lasts around two or three hours on December 20th, and includes snacks at the afterparty
    • Megameetup is a full weekend from evening of December 19th to morning of December 21st, and includes three meals
    • The overnight rooms are dorms with bunk beds.

    Kids 13 and under can come to megameetup for free as long as they have a parent or guardian attending the megameetup. We don't as of right now have any childcare or children's programming, but we're working on it. Please email us at east.coast.rationalist.megameetup@gmail.com if you'd like to help with that effort.

    If you're interested in sponsoring the event, also please email me; I'm happy to discuss options.
