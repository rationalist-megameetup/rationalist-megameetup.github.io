    Payment

    • If you want to come to Solstice, you want a Secular Solstice Ticket.
    • If you want to come to Megameetup, you want a Megameetup Ticket.
    • If you want to come to both, you want both.
    • If you have either, you're welcome at the afterparty.
    • If you're planning to sleep overnight at the venue, we have a hotel block here. If you want to find roommates to split the cost, you can do that in the Discord. If you need a different room configuration, you can book with the hotel— please let them know you're with the Rationalist Megameetup!
    • Kids 13 and under can come to megameetup for free as long as they have a parent or guardian attending the megameetup. We don't have any childcare or children's programming.
    • Once you've paid, please fill out the registration form!
