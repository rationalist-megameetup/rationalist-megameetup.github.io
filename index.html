    Secular Solstice & Rationalist Megameetup 2023

    On December 9th, at Ida Lang theatre, New York City will have a Secular Solstice. Solstice is a holiday designed by and for rationalists. Secular Solstices take place in many cities, but the tradition started in New York and for me there's no better place for it. Now a tradition spanning over a decade, rationalists from across North America- and sometimes from further afield- will come together to sing about the distant past and the hoped for future.

    From the evening of December 8th to the morning of December 11th, NYC will also be the home of the NYC Rationalist Megameetup. We will have sleeping space (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday nights) for those from out of town as well as meeting spaces to congregate in. This year the Rationalist Megameetup will take place in the Brooklyn Downtown Holiday Inn at 300 Schermerhorn Street, Brooklyn. Historically the Megameetup has had attendee-led estimation games, impromptu sing-alongs, and the highlighted lightning talks. That said, the Megameetup that can be named is not the true Megameetup. In previous years it felt like every corner I turned had people talking excitedly about topics I couldn't find anywhere outside of a rationalist meetup.

    Six years ago, the first megameetup was an experiment. It wasn't clear at the time that this was something people wanted, and it was a smaller event. The result of that experiment was positive, and every year the venue filled to capacity. Now we're trying another experiment; since the most common piece of feedback is that the venue was too small we've scaled up from a large apartment to a small hotel. This means it's more expensive, but also means we have a lot more space.

    Another change: This year, Solstice and Megameetup registrations are combined, though you can of course register for just solstice if you like. Use the payment page to pick which combination of Solstice, Megameetup events, and overnight sleeping arrangements you prefer. (If you want all three, pick one of each kind!) Once you do, an email should automatically send with the rest of the registration!

    I hope to see you there!

    -Skyler Crossman
