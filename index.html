    East Coast Rationalist Megameetup 2019

    NYC Secular Solstice this year wason December the 21st, Saturday Night, in the Ida Long auditorium at Hunter College. That weekend, from Friday December 20th to Monday December 23rd, we hosted a weekend long rationalist community get-together with accommodations for those who were visiting.

    That event is over, and thank you to everyone who helped out throughout the weekend, from making food to helping clean up before we left and so much more. Thank you as well to everyone who attended. If you'd like, a survey is below.

    In the event you need to contact the organizer that is the surest way to do so, though I'll no longer be closely monitoring that address. Still, it should eventually get into the hands of me or a successor.

    -Skyler Crossman, 26th of December, 2019
