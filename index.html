    East Coast Rationalist Megameetup 2022

    Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, no megameetup was held in 2020 or 2021. We're going to try and hold one in 2022.

    The current intention is to hold a megameetup in December to coincide with the NYC solstice. To our knowledge, which weekend solstice will be held on has not yet been determined. Once we know what weekend to aim at, we'll announce that here and make arrangements to welcome you all to NYC.

    Speaking personally: I've missed these far too much to let them fall by the wayside another year.

    -Skyler Crossman, 25th of April, 2022
